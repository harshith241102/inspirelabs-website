Operator governance

Judgment stays human-led.

Speed matters. But accountability matters more. These six areas define where human operators remain the decision-makers.

Operators decide strategy, brand fit, partner handling, quality standards, and what action should follow from the data. AI supports the work. Operators remain accountable for outcomes.

01

Strategy review

Operators set the direction, review priorities, and approve strategic plans before execution begins.

02

Quality control

Human review checks content, data accuracy, campaign setup, and output quality before anything goes live.

03

Partner alignment

Operators manage brand relationships, partner expectations, and communication with direct accountability.

04

Brand safety

Operators enforce brand guidelines, compliance checks, and content standards across all outputs.

05

Reporting interpretation

Operators interpret data, add context, identify what matters, and decide what action follows from the numbers.

06

Outcome accountability

Operators own the results. AI improves the workflow, but responsibility stays with the team.
