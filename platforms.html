Flagship Consumer-intent surface Live since 2013

GrabOn

GrabOn captures high-intent savings and commerce behavior across India, global, and UAE properties. It is the primary consumer-intent surface in the Inspirelabs system, generating the intent signals that feed growth platforms, AI workflows, and partner distribution.

Capabilities

  • Coupon and deal aggregation across 3,800+ brands
  • Category-level commerce discovery (fashion, electronics, food, travel)
  • Store-level savings pages with verified offers
  • Multi-property operation (India, Global, UAE)
  • Mobile apps on Play Store and App Store
  • Brand and merchant partnership network

96M+ transactions/year

Affiliate Earnings Growth platform

GrabCash

GrabCash is the performance affiliate layer of the Inspirelabs system. It enables students, homemakers, and everyday creators to share verified deals with their network and earn a real cut. It turns social distribution into measurable growth for brands while giving users an income channel built on trust, not follower counts.

Capabilities

  • Affiliate link generation with server-side attribution
  • Real-time earnings dashboard for creators
  • Direct bank withdrawal for earned commissions
  • Curated deal catalog from verified brand partners
  • Social sharing tools for WhatsApp, Instagram, and more
  • Performance analytics per link and campaign

Proof

5,000+ active affiliates
Server-side attribution model

Ecosystem role

What GrabCash feeds into the system

Distributed social commerce signals, affiliate conversion data, and real-world demand validation that helps brands understand which offers drive action beyond the platform.

Creator layer Growth platform

GrabShare

GrabShare is a creator-led storefront where verified voices curate the products they actually use. It connects social commerce with the way people actually discover and buy, turning trusted recommendations into measurable brand growth through revenue-share mechanics.

Capabilities

  • Creator storefronts with curated product collections
  • Smart link generation for monetized recommendations
  • Revenue-share model with transparent commission tracking
  • Brand collaboration tools for sponsored placements
  • Creator analytics dashboard with earning insights
  • Mobile-first experience designed for social sharing

Ecosystem role

What GrabShare feeds into the system

Creator-led purchase signals, social proof data, and authentic product recommendations that complement the intent captured by consumer surfaces.

Alternatives.co

Alternatives.co is a consumer-intent surface for software discovery. It captures comparison and evaluation behavior from users researching tools, platforms, and business software. It serves a growing segment of high-intent decision-makers looking for the right product for their needs.

Capabilities

  • Side-by-side product comparison tools
  • Category-level software discovery across all verticals
  • Alternative recommendations based on user needs
  • Verified user reviews and ratings
  • GPT-powered search for software recommendations

System flow

Intent flows through the system. Outcomes improve with every loop.

Intent is captured

Consumer-intent surfaces like GrabOn and Alternatives.co capture user behavior at the moment of discovery, comparison, and purchase intent.

Growth is activated

Growth platforms like GrabCash and GrabShare turn captured intent into distribution through cashback, social sharing, and creator-led commerce.

Intelligence sharpens execution

Supporting platforms like RankDrive and WriteGenius improve search visibility, content quality, and operating speed across every surface.

Data feeds back into the system

Every transaction, search, and interaction improves targeting, content relevance, and partner matching across the entire platform network.

Outcomes compound

The system gets smarter with every loop. Growth compounds because each platform strengthens the others instead of operating in isolation.
